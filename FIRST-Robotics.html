Rookie Season

In our rookie season (2019), we won Rookie All-Star at a regional tournament, qualifying us to the World Championship in Houston, TX. It was a week of fun, competing against the best in the world, and we ended up placing 25th (of 68) in our division.

FIRST Tech Challenge

Before our school had an FRC team, we had two FTC teams. In FTC, the robots are smaller and significantly easier to make. This helped us build a strong foundation before joining FRC, and was my introduction to the world of robotics.

Gracious Professionalism

Gracious professionalism is a cornerstone of FIRST Robotics. Invented by the late Woodie Flowers of MIT, GP is all about competing for the sake of bettering everyone involved.
