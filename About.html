About Me

Through sheer luck I discovered my high school's robotics club. Once I got my driver's license, I was practically living in the shop! I fell in love with all aspects of robotics—designing, fabricating, assembling, wiring, and coding.

Since then, I've continued to dedicate time to my passions, always hungry to learn more about what excites me. I can't call myself a programmer because I don't just write code. I'm not an EE, MechE, or woodworker. I just like to make stuff.
